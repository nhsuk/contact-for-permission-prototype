What this service is

Use this service to give permission (consent) for researchers to contact you about taking part in clinical trials for a coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccine.

Find out more about taking part in clinical trials at the Be Part of Research website.

Who can use this service

You can use this service if:

  • you are 18 or over, and
  • you live in the UK

What this service will ask for

We will ask for some information about you, including your:

  • date of birth
  • name
  • email address
  • postcode
  • sex registered at birth
  • gender
  • ethnic group
  • health conditions you may have
  • type of work

We ask this so we can get the right mix of different people on clinical trials.

Then we will ask for your permission to contact you.
