What this service is

Use this service to give permission (consent) for researchers to contact you about taking part in clinical trials for a coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccine.

Who can use this service

You can use this service if:

  • you are 18 or over
  • and you live in the UK

What this service will ask for

We'll ask you some questions including:

  • your name
  • date of birth
  • email address
  • postcode

Then we'll ask for your permission to contact you.
