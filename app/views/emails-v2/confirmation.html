What happens next

Researchers will contact you if they think you may be suitable for a coronavirus clinical trial. Researchers may be from the NHS, or other places like drug companies or universities.

There is no guarantee you will be contacted to be part of a clinical trial. You may not be contacted if researchers have enough of a particular mix of people, not be because of your personal or health information.

If you gave permission, NHS Digital will also contact you about coronavirus prevention, treatment and other health research when more information is available.

More information

You can find out more information about the different types of research studies and trials on the Be Part of Research website.
